Remarks:

Richard Smeal Jr's last known residences include Waukee and Adel, Iowa.

Caution:

Richard Smeal Jr. is wanted for allegedly aiding his secret lover to defraud "Cooperate Property Services INC," a real estate developer in deerfield beach ,Florida to lose $7,750. The CEO of "cooperate property services INC." sent us a letter requesting legal action. On April 1st 2024, a federal arrest warrant was issued for Richard Smeal Jr in the United States Supreme Court, District of Columbia ,after he was charged with Check Fraud and MoneyLaundering.

 

On March 19, 2024, a federal arrest warrant was issued for Richard Smeal in the United States District Court, Southern District of Iowa, Des Moines, Iowa, after he was charged with Wire Fraud and Money Laundering.
